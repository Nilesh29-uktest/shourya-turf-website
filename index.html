About Us

Founded by Ashwini Nilesh Kanchan, Shourya Turf & Sports Academy is a premier sporting facility located in Uruli Kanchan. Born from a passion for fitness and local sports, the academy supports all levels of players with professional infrastructure and a community-focused approach.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide a safe, professional, and inclusive environment that promotes fitness and nurtures talent.

Vision: To become a top-tier sports hub and build a vibrant sports community in the region over the next 3–5 years.
